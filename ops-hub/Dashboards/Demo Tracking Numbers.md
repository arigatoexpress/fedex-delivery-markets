# 📦 Demo Tracking Numbers

These are **fake** tracking numbers built into the app for demos. They are **not
real packages.** Copy/paste them to avoid typos.

| Tracking number | State | What you'll see | Use it to show… |
|---|---|---|---|
| `771234567890` | **Pre-hub** | Markets **open** — you can take a side | A live, open YES/NO market + quoting + a paper order |
| `882345678901` | **Hub-arrived** | Markets **locked** — no new sides | What "locked" looks like once a package hits the hub |
| `993456789012` | **Delivered** | Markets **resolved** — final answer | A finished market with its YES/NO outcome |

> [!tip] Suggested demo order
> Start with `771234567890` (open, interactive), then jump to `993456789012`
> (resolved) to "close the loop." Use `882345678901` only if asked about the
> in-between "locked" state. Full script: [[Run a Demo (step by step)]].

> [!warning] Never type a *real* tracking number into this app.
> It's paper-only and uses synthetic data only. If you're ever prompted for
> something real, stop and see [[Incident & Escalation]].

> [!tip] See it on a route
> The [[Delivery Route Map.excalidraw|🚚 Delivery Route Map]] shows exactly where
> each of these numbers sits — open before the hub, locked at the hub, resolved on
> delivery.
