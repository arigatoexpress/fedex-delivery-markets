# 👋 Welcome to the Delivery Markets Lab — Ops Hub

This is your **plain-English handbook** for the FedEx **Delivery Markets Lab**.
You do **not** need to be technical. Everything here is written for ops managers.

> [!important] The one thing to remember
> **This is a paper-only demo.** No real money, no real customer data, no real
> tracking, no live trading. It exists to *learn and to start governance
> conversations* — not to run a business yet. See [[Safety & What This Is NOT]].
